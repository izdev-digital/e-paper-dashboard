Privacy Policy

What We Collect

We store your username, display name, and dashboard configurations locally to authenticate your account and provide personalized dashboards.

Data Usage

  • Your data is stored locally and never shared with third parties
  • Cookies are used only for authentication
  • You can delete your account and data at any time from your profile settings

⚠️ Security Note

This application runs over HTTP by default. For production use, configure HTTPS to encrypt data in transit.
