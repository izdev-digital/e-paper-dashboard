Privacy Policy

Information We Collect & How We Use It

We collect your username, display name, and dashboard configurations to authenticate your account and provide a personalized experience. Your data is stored locally and used only for dashboard functionality—never shared with third parties.

Data Protection

  • No Third-Party Sharing: Your data is never shared externally
  • Authentication Only: Cookies are used only for session management
  • HTTPS Recommended: Enable HTTPS with SSL/TLS for production deployments

⚠️ Security Note

This application runs over HTTP by default (unencrypted). For production use, configure HTTPS with a valid SSL/TLS certificate.

Your Rights

  • Contact your administrator with privacy questions or concerns
